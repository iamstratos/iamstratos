Enjoy the power of touch.

Combining multitouch and 3D sensing like never before, Haptix is the
next-generation controller for your computer. See where your fingers are in
mid-air, then simply tap to click, pinch to zoom, or swipe to scroll from any surface.

Haptix is the first device to enable both multitouch and 3D sensing while
remaining affordable, small, and not reliant on infrared (Sun? What sun?). It’s
as natural as a multitouch screen, just without the screen.

Don’t be a trafifc cop.

You know what we’re talking about. Traditional gesture control is about
holding your hand in the air – like a traffic cop. It’s also why multitouch
laptops can be tiring to use. Instead, Haptix is about truly natural interaction.
It’s about resting your hands on a surface to use a computer. It’s about
touching something solid to click on links.

For example, clip Haptix on top of your laptop to turn your keyboard into a
multitouch surface. Your hands no longer have to constantly shuffle to the
mouse/trackpad, and typing isn’t a problem either. It’s like reaching out and
touching the screen, except your hands are well-rested in one spot. And
that’s the beauty of Haptix.
