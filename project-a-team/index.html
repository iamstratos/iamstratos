Reasons to join

Excellence and Team Spirit

At Project A, you will work together with experienced specialists in the areas of business intelligence, software development, product management, online marketing, human resources, design, sales and communication. We value individual performance just as highly as team spirit and believe firmly that the best idea is the one that should be realized – no matter whether it comes from an intern or our managing director.

We work hard to promote solidarity and a sense of community at Project A, among other ways by organising tons of events and activities, such as regular team events, parties and sports and fitness groups.

Never Stop Learning

As an operative VC, the knowledge of our experts is our most important capital. It drives our ventures forward and shapes the future of our branch. We support the further development of our employees by providing opportunities to participate in further training, workshops and conferences, as well as offering our own formats within the company across the breadth of our portfolio to promote the interdisciplinary exchange of knowledge.

We are constantly on the lookout for qualified applicants who would like to develop themselves and grow with us. The dynamic nature of the sector we operate in and our own expanding portfolio mean that new career possibilities are always opening up.

Open Company Culture with Strong Values

Transparency and an open exchange of ideas are essential for the success of our business – both within our teams and across all departments. By holding weekly “all-hands” meetings, an annual strategy day and regular portfolio reviews, together we gain an overview of current issues. Here everyone can contribute their own suggestions and ideas and play an active role in shaping our company culture. That’s why we also encourage everyone to take part in our regular feedback processes.

The exchange of knowledge, mutual respect, dependability and a pragmatic approach to work are several of the core values that unite us as a team.

Many Other Benefits

From muesli and fresh fruit, to good coffee and a variety of beverages, all the way to the annual flu vaccination – you’ll be well taken care of with us. On top of that, our lounge, several outdoor terraces and our game room offer space for diverting breaks and casual meetings. Last not least, you will be able to take advantage of a number of gift certificates and discounts that our ventures make available to us.
