Manage your lists

You can see all your lists and export them all at once. Also, you can add or remove one, anytime you want. Don’t forget the indicators on the right, they’ll always show you how many saved tasks you have.

You should mouse over a website to see ‘export’ & ‘remove’ option.

Add, edit or remove tasks easily

Edit a task by clicking on it. Then, you can either change it or remove it. Would you like to add a new task? Just start typing (or hit the gray field on top).

We will automatically unckeck your task when you’re done.

Export your favourite lists

You can export your lists to several services. Manage your tasks and keep your website lists together with a few clicks. In addition, you can choose other formats and services that we don’t support.

‘Other format...’ option allows you to pick a variety of services.
